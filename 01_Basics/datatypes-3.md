Let's try to Know about str






String (str):

- used to store text / characters
- collection of characters enclosed in quotes
- quotes: ' ' , " " , ''' ''' , """ """

example: "Aman", 'Python'


Types:

- Single line → 'Hello' , "Hello"
- Multi line → '''Hello''' , """Hello"""

- strings are immutable (cannot change after creation)


s1 = "Python"
s2 = 'Aman'

print(type(s1))
print(type(s2))

World"""
